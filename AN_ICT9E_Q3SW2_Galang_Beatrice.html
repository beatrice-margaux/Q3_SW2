MOVIE REVIEW

Eerie

Directed by Mikhail Red

Realeased on March 27, 2019

A guidance counsellor's special talents help her unravel the mystery behind the unexpected and gruesome death of a student at a Catholic school for girls.

RATING:

10/10 - The movie was spectacular. Its plot and the stories of each character were so well-written. It was scary--as promised--but what the characters were going through gave an emotional mix to to. Great production and great acting.

Deleter

Directed by Mikhail Red

Realeased on December 25, 2022

An online content moderator deletes a suicide video made by her co-worker, but the otherwise desensitized woman can't escape her own troubled past or a mysterious vengeful presence.

RATING:

9/10 - This movie's plot was amazing. The storyline is great, but I do think that the movie ended in a blur. Other than that issue, the acting, the story, and the production was spectacular. I would pay to watch this movie for the first time again.

Clarita

Directed by Roderick Cabrido

Realeased on June 12, 2019

A young woman, Clarita Villanueva, is held against her will when police suspect she has been possessed by a demon. Perhaps, multiple of them.

RATING:

9.5/10 - The movie, the acting, everything was amazing. I admire how they did not focus the entire story on Clarita. The priests had their stories, the nun had her story. It's a great way to reenact what happened to Clarita Villanueva.
